  • What if my company wasn’t founded in the last 4 years?

    First of all, congratulations! If you’ve made it this far, you’re definitely doing something right. Unfortunately, in order to qualify for free Kindling, organizations have to be less than four years old at the time of their application. The good news: it’s still easy and affordable for longer-running startups to use Kindling. To learn more, view Kindling’s pricing.

  • What if my startup has more than 50 employees, or we grow, or we get bought?

    That’s the goal, right? Hopefully, Kindling will have played a part in that success. Your first 50 seats are always free, and if you grow, we’ll give you all the help you need to get the most out of an expanded Kindling account. Check out our pricing and contact us to add more seats.

  • How long are my 50 seats free?

    F-O-R-E-V-E-R. Forever. No strings attached.

  • Why are you doing this?

    As a startup ourselves, we understand how critical it is to engage your employees and customers in shaping your product or service. You just don't have the resources to work towards realizing the wrong ideas. Tim describes our motivations further in his blog post announcing Kindling Loves Startups.

  • Do I have to be a “tech” startup to qualify?

    No way! The only requirements is that your startup be founded in the last 4 years.

  • Who else uses Kindling?

    We have nearly 100 large-scale customers, and are growing fast. Check out what some of our favorite customers have to say about Kindling. Or, see how Emma used Kindling to grow itself as a startup in this case study.

    Kindling provides a place to make suggestions, discuss ideas and move conversations ahead in a way that keeps people engaged and turns ideas into action. In addition, Kindling offers an API for integrating with existing services. Learn more about Kindling’s unique features.
